About me

Hey there! I'm Jan, a full-stack developer-in-the-making with a passion for both code and community. Currently, I'm diving deep into React and JavaScript at my technical college in Kraków, where I've been honing my programming skills since 2019. My journey started with Godot and its scripting language, GDScript, and over the past five years, I've explored various languages and technologies, always eager to learn and grow.

But coding isn't my only forte. I'm also passionate about leadership and problem-solving. As the project manager for the Rock N’ Aid Kraków Underground Festival, I led a team to organize a charity concert aimed at supporting children in need. The event featured local rock bands and raised funds for SOS Children's Villages, showcasing the power of music to make a difference.

Beyond the stage, I serve as a counselor with the Młodzieżowa Rada Krakowa (Youth Council of Kraków), where I collaborate with peers to represent youth interests and engage in civic initiatives. This role has sharpened my teamwork, communication, and organizational skills, all while staying connected to the community.

Whether it's debugging a tricky piece of code or coordinating a community event, I approach challenges with a positive mindset and a collaborative spirit. I'm always looking for opportunities to learn, lead, and contribute to meaningful projects.
